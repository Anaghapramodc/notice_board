About Sir Syed Institute for Technical Studies

Sir Syed Institute for Technical Studies is a prestigious project of Cannanore District Muslim Educational Association (C.D.M.E.A) which also runs Sir Syed College, Keyi Sahib Training College, Sir Syed Higher Secondary School and Kurumathur South U.P. School.

Sir Syed Institute for Technical Studies in Taliparamba, established in 2002, is an educational institution dedicated to providing technical education and fostering intellectual growth among its students.

Through its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and comprehensive curriculum, the Sir Syed Institute for Technical Studies endeavors to empower its students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their chosen fields.

The Institute offers 4 PG courses and 13 UG courses. The college is affiliated to Kannur University and approved by the Government of Kerala.

The Institute provides individual hostel accommodations for both boys and girls. The college has about 1500 students, 60 teaching staff and 20 non-teaching staff. The students are expected to come to the College in a prescribed uniform.

The College has zero tolerance towards ragging. Students are expected to strictly adhere to the code of conduct prescribed by the institution.

The Sir Syed Institute for Technical Studies in Taliparamba is dedicated to providing its students with a well-rounded educational experience that goes beyond academics.

Additionally, the college office is proactive in assisting students in accessing scholarships and endowments, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their academic pursuits.
